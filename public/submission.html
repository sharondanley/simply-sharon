Capture Your Best Selfie

  1. Wear your regular makeup and hairstyle, keep hair away from your brows, and remove glasses.
  2. Using a selfie stick, take a head-and-shoulders photo only with a clean background and no ceiling or room distractions.
  3. Many phones automatically add filters, so please check your camera settings and remove them before taking the photo.
  4. Stand facing a window, but not in direct sunlight, to avoid hot spots and shadows while keeping the image clear.
  5. Keep your chin parallel to the floor, face straight ahead with no angle, and position the camera lens at eye level while looking directly into it.
  6. Show a pleasant expression, smiling slightly with both your eyes and your mouth.
